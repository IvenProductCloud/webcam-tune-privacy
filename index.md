# Privacy Policy

**Webcam Tune**
Last updated: March 29, 2026

## Overview

Webcam Tune is a macOS application developed by Iven that provides real-time control over USB webcam settings. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Webcam Tune does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — The app contains no analytics SDKs or tracking code.
- **No network requests** — The app operates entirely offline. It never connects to the internet.
- **No accounts or registration** — No sign-up or login is required.
- **No data shared with third parties** — Nothing is sent to us or anyone else.

## Camera Access

Webcam Tune requires access to your camera to display a live preview and adjust camera settings. Camera data is processed locally in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

## Local Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as camera presets and display settings) locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

Webcam Tune does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
