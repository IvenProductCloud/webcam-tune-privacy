## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why don't I see any controls for my camera?**
Webcam Tune works with external USB webcams that support UVC controls. Built-in cameras (like FaceTime HD) and Continuity Camera do not expose adjustable controls.

**Why does a slider not seem to do anything?**
Some cameras don't support every UVC control, even if it appears in the list. Try adjusting other controls to find the ones your camera responds to.

**How do I save my settings?**
Use the Presets feature to save and quickly switch between different camera configurations.
